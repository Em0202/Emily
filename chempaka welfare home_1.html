About Us

Chemapka Welfare Home Malaysia is a non-profit organization established in 2005 with the mission to provide a safe and nurturing home for abandoned, unwanted, and abused children in Malaysia. We are fully registered with the Department of Social Welfare Malaysia and operate under the supervision of the Ministry of Women, Family and Community Development. Our motto is to provide hope, love, and care to our children so they can grow into responsible adults and make a positive contribution to society.

Activities

At Chemapka Welfare Home Malaysia, we provide a wide range of activities and programs for our children to help them achieve their full potential. Here are some of the activities we offer:

  • Education and tutoring services
  • Sports and recreation programs
  • Arts and crafts workshops
  • Computer and coding classes
  • Outdoor activities and camping trips

We also organize cultural and religious events to help our children learn and appreciate their heritage and beliefs.

Achievements

Over the years, Chemapka Welfare Home Malaysia has achieved many important milestones:

  • Provided a safe and loving home to over 1000 children
  • Provided education and tutoring services to hundreds of children
  • Organized numerous sports, arts, and cultural events
  • Received recognition and support from local and international organizations
  • Helped many of our children to graduate from universities and colleges

We are grateful for the generosity of our donors, volunteers, and sponsors who have made our achievements possible.
